What We Do

HOW WE WORK

The Allison Foundation provides assistance to people & families in need. How we contribute is based on the needs of a community as defined by its most precious resource-the people. We use your support to empower world changers and difference makers leading their communities in providing various giving opportunities, running food programs, creating a safe place for children, & assisting in emergencies. Everything we do is to bring hope in the Uintah Basin.

OUR GOAL

Our mission at The Allison Foundation is to provide the Uintah Basin's children and families in need to get the help they need. We don’t just want to help them succeed in the future, but we also want everyone feel loved at hard times especially as a community.
